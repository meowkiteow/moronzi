Our Company

Strategic advisory for the offshore marine sector.

Muronzi Inc is a U.S.-registered strategic offshore marine advisory and commercial interface firm focused on structured alignment between vessel owners, licensed brokers, and offshore energy stakeholders.

Operating from Washington, DC and New York, the company provides governance-based commercial structuring and cross-jurisdictional engagement support for multi-asset marine initiatives. Rather than operating vessels or acting as contractor, Muronzi Inc functions as a strategic intermediary — facilitating disciplined commercial alignment across offshore marine programs.
