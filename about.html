Executive Leadership

Muronzi Inc

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

2023 – Present | United States

Founded in 2023, Muronzi Inc is a U.S.-registered strategic advisory and commercial interface firm operating at the intersection of offshore marine coordination and structured energy-sector alignment.

The company does not own or operate vessels. Instead, Muronzi Inc functions as a strategic commercial interface — facilitating engagement between vessel owners, licensed brokerage entities, and offshore contractors under governance-aware, long-term charter frameworks.

Current focus includes:

  • Offshore marine asset alignment (AHTS, PSV, FSIV, Tugs, Barges, Crew Boats, OIMR)
  • Long-term charter structuring (5+2 frameworks)
  • Cross-border maritime transaction coordination
  • Institutional positioning within regulated offshore markets
  • Risk-managed commercial interface advisory

Programs currently being positioned target synchronized Q3 2026 deployment under multi-year charter structures.
