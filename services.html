Our Services

Advisory-level marine commercial services structured around governance, alignment, and disciplined engagement. We do not operate vessels or act as contractors — we facilitate strategic commercial outcomes.

01

Offshore Program Structuring

Commercial structuring and stakeholder coordination for multi-asset offshore marine initiatives within energy-sector frameworks.

Offshore energy programs require precise commercial coordination between multiple parties — vessel owners, energy operators, regulatory bodies, and financial institutions. Muronzi Inc provides the strategic structuring layer that brings these stakeholders into alignment.

We work alongside licensed brokerage entities to develop commercially viable program structures for multi-vessel offshore deployments. This includes defining charter frameworks, identifying stakeholder requirements, establishing commercial terms, and ensuring governance compliance across all participating entities.

Our role is not operational — we do not deploy vessels or manage field operations. Instead, we structure the commercial architecture that enables offshore programs to proceed under disciplined, transparent, and governance-aligned frameworks.

Scope of Advisory

  • Multi-asset program commercial architecture
  • Stakeholder alignment and coordination
  • Charter framework development
  • Governance and compliance structuring
  • Cross-party commercial term negotiation support

02

Vessel Owner Interface

Facilitating structured commercial dialogue between vessel owners, licensed brokers, and offshore project entities.

Vessel owners seeking charter opportunities in the offshore energy sector face a complex landscape of intermediaries, regulatory requirements, and commercial structures. Muronzi Inc serves as a strategic interface — connecting vessel owners with qualified charterers through disciplined commercial channels.

We do not act as principals or take positions on vessels. Rather, we facilitate introductions, structure initial commercial dialogue, and support the development of charter terms that protect both parties. All engagement is conducted alongside licensed brokerage entities who manage the transactional and regulatory components.

Our value lies in relationship architecture — understanding the requirements of both vessel owners and energy operators, and structuring the commercial conversation in a way that leads to executable agreements.

Scope of Advisory

  • Vessel owner engagement and introduction
  • Charterer qualification and matching
  • Commercial dialogue facilitation
  • Term sheet structuring support
  • Relationship management across parties

03

Charter Advisory & Alignment

Advisory participation in long-term charter frameworks and commission-based marine asset arrangements.

Long-term charter agreements in the offshore marine sector are high-value, multi-year commitments that require careful structuring, due diligence, and commercial alignment. Muronzi Inc provides advisory-level support throughout the charter development process.

We advise on charter structure — time-charter, bareboat, and hybrid arrangements — helping parties identify the most appropriate framework for their specific operational and financial requirements. Our advisory extends to term negotiation, rate benchmarking, and risk allocation guidance.

Muronzi participates in charter arrangements on a commission advisory basis, working alongside licensed entities who manage the formal brokerage components. This ensures full regulatory compliance while providing clients with strategic commercial insight.

Scope of Advisory

  • Charter structure advisory (time-charter, bareboat, hybrid)
  • Commercial term alignment
  • Rate benchmarking and market positioning
  • Risk allocation and contract structuring guidance
  • Commission-based advisory participation

04

Marine Commercial Structuring

Governance-sensitive advisory support across U.S. and international marine regulatory environments.

Marine commercial transactions operate within a complex web of national and international regulations — including U.S. maritime law, flag state requirements, classification society standards, and environmental compliance frameworks. Muronzi Inc provides governance-sensitive commercial structuring advisory that accounts for these regulatory realities.

We do not provide legal counsel. Our role is commercial structuring — ensuring that proposed marine transactions, charter programs, and stakeholder arrangements are structured in a way that is commercially viable and governance-aligned.

This is particularly critical for cross-border marine initiatives where multiple regulatory jurisdictions intersect. We help stakeholders navigate the commercial implications of regulatory requirements, ensuring that deal structures remain robust across all applicable frameworks.

Scope of Advisory

  • Governance-aligned commercial structuring
  • Cross-jurisdictional regulatory navigation
  • Compliance-sensitive deal architecture
  • Multi-party commercial framework development
  • Regulatory impact assessment for commercial terms

Energy-Sector Marine Advisory

Strategic commercial positioning support for marine stakeholders engaging in offshore energy programs.

The offshore energy sector — oil, gas, wind, and subsea infrastructure — requires specialized marine assets and commercially sophisticated engagement structures. Muronzi Inc advises marine stakeholders on strategic commercial positioning within this sector.

For vessel owners, we provide insight into energy operator requirements, deployment timelines, and commercial expectations — helping them position their assets for engagement in offshore energy programs. Our energy-sector advisory is rooted in commercial intelligence — understanding market dynamics, rate environments, fleet availability, and program timelines. This intelligence informs the strategic positioning advice we provide to both sides of the marine-energy interface.

06

Brokerage Collaboration

Structured advisory participation alongside licensed marine brokerage entities under compliant engagement models.

Muronzi Inc does not operate as a standalone brokerage. Instead, we work alongside licensed marine brokerage entities — providing strategic advisory, stakeholder access, and commercial structuring support within compliant engagement models.

This collaboration model allows us to add value at the strategic and commercial level while ensuring that all transactional, regulatory, and compliance responsibilities are managed by appropriately licensed entities. Our participation is structured under formal agreements — including MOUs and commission-sharing arrangements — that define clear roles, responsibilities, and governance frameworks.

For licensed brokers, Muronzi provides access to strategic stakeholder networks, commercial structuring expertise, and governance-aligned deal architecture. For clients, this collaboration model ensures that transactions benefit from both specialized brokerage capability and strategic commercial advisory.

Scope of Advisory

  • Licensed brokerage partnership facilitation
  • MOU and commission-sharing structure development
  • Stakeholder network access
  • Compliance-aligned engagement models
  • Strategic commercial overlay for brokerage transactions
